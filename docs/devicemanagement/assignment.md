# Assignment

The asset assignment for a user or device.

**Platforms:** Device Assignment Services , VPP License Management 

## Properties

### adamId

- **Type:** `string`
- **Required:** No

The unique identifier for a product in the store.

### clientUserId

- **Type:** `string`
- **Required:** No

The unique identifier for an active user in your organization.

### pricingParam

- **Type:** `string`
- **Required:** No
- **Allowed Values:** `STDQ`, `PLUS`

The quality of a product in the store.

### serialNumber

- **Type:** `string`
- **Required:** No

The unique identifier for a device in your organization.
